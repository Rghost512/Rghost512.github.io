My Go-To Picks

These are a few picks I like using depending on what I’m playing. I’m using Bootstrap cards here to keep the layout clean and easy to scan.

Dunlop Tortex (Orange)

Good all-around pick. It’s grippy, consistent, and works for rhythm and lead without feeling too stiff.

Medium feel

Dunlop Jazz III

Smaller pick with more control. I like this one for tighter picking and lead lines when I want precision.

Precise control

Fender Medium

Classic feel. This one is comfortable and simple, and it works when I’m just playing casually.

Comfort pick
